Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Shutterstock Pro

Is Shutterstock Pro really free?

Yes! It's completely free and open source. No paid tiers, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. Created by Trovant Studio (a Dipto Design brand) for the creative community.

Does it work on all Shutterstock pages?

Yes! It works on any Shutterstock asset page (shutterstock.com/image-photo/..., shutterstock.com/image-vector/...). The extension activates automatically when you visit Shutterstock.

How does the auto-expand feature work?

When you click "Copy Title/Description", the extension automatically finds and clicks any "See more" buttons to expand the full description. It then copies the complete text, not just the truncated preview.

What keywords does it copy?

It copies keywords from multiple sources: keyword links, keyword tags, and Shutterstock-specific containers. It intelligently filters out UI elements and duplicates.

How are keywords formatted?

Keywords are copied as a comma-separated list (e.g., "sunset, mountains, lake"). This format works perfectly for pasting into search fields, spreadsheets, or keyword management tools.

Does it collect my data?

No! The extension works completely locally. No data is sent to any server. Your keywords and browsing stay private. The only permission needed is to access Shutterstock pages.
