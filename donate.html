Donation FAQ

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Clify is a personal open-source project, not a registered non-profit. Donations are not tax-deductible in most countries. However, your support helps keep the project alive and growing!

Where does my money go?

100% of donations go towards server costs ($20/month), development tools, bug bounties, and compensating translators. I don't take a salary - this is a passion project.

How do I donate via bKash/Nagad?

Select your preferred mobile banking method above. Send any amount via "Send Money" to the provided number (01627588083 for most services, 017855101573 for Rocket, 016275880839 for MCash). Use "CLIFY" as reference. After sending, you can optionally email us for acknowledgment.

How do I donate via Payoneer?

Send payment to diptodas1998bd@gmail.com via Payoneer. Include "CLIFY" in the notes so we can acknowledge your support.

How do I donate via nSave?

Open nSave app and send to @dipto_das. Use reference "CLIFY". Thank you for your support!

How do I donate via bank transfer?

Use the Islami Bank Bangladesh PLC details provided above. Account Name: DIPTO DAS, Account Number: 20507770206430163, Routing: 125270607. Send from any bank in Bangladesh.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes! Use cryptocurrency, choose "anonymous" on donation platforms, or donate via mobile banking without sharing your name. Your name won't appear in the supporters list.
