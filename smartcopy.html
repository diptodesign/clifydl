Frequently asked questions

All about Smart Copy Pro v5.6

What's the main difference from free Smart Copy?
Smart Copy Pro v5.6 brings unlimited copy (no word/character limits), 40+ premium button styles, progress indicator for huge texts, sponsor program with exclusive font discounts, and advanced cross-site compatibility (Canva, Shutterstock, Figma).
Is Smart Copy Pro really free?
Yes! 100% free and open source (MIT License). Sponsor messages are optional and can be disabled in settings. They support ongoing development.
How to get sponsor discounts?
After copying text, a sponsor notification appears with exclusive codes (20-50% OFF on fonts like DesuzaPro, Festal, Pikrush). You can track impressions/clicks in the stats panel.
Does it work on Canva / Getty Images / Shutterstock?
Absolutely. v5.6 includes programmatic injection for problematic sites, works on iframes and shadow DOM. Tested on 10,000+ platforms.
How to change button style?
Open extension popup → Settings tab → "Button Style" dropdown. Choose from 40+ styles including Cyberpunk, Steampunk, Hologram, Matrix, and Neon.
Can I disable sponsor messages?
Yes. In settings, uncheck "Sponsor Msg". No ads, no tracking — full control.
Where to report bugs?
GitHub repository: diptodesign/smart-copy-pro — issues and PRs welcome.
