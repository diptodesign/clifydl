🛡️ Our Commitment to Privacy

ScrollUp is designed with a zero-data-collection philosophy. Your privacy is not just a policy—it's built into the extension's core.

📊 Data Collection

ScrollUp collects NO data whatsoever. This includes:

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Website content
  • Usage statistics
  • IP addresses
  • Device information
  • Cookies or trackers

🔧 Required Permissions

Permission Why It's Needed
storage Saves your settings (position, button size, reverse scroll, branding preference) locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser.
<all_urls> Allows the scroll button to appear on every website you visit. The extension does not read, modify, or interact with website content in any way.

💾 How Your Settings Are Stored

  • ✓ Settings are saved using Chrome's storage.sync API
  • ✓ Data is stored locally on your device
  • ✓ If you're signed into Chrome, settings sync across your browsers (encrypted by Chrome)
  • ✓ No data is ever sent to our servers (because we don't have any!)

🌐 Third-Party Services

ScrollUp does NOT use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Any tracking pixels
  • Advertising networks
  • External APIs
  • Any third-party services at all

📅 If this policy ever changes (it probably won't, since we don't collect data), we'll update the "Last Updated" date and notify users via the GitHub repository.
