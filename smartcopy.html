Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is Smart Copy free?
Yes! Smart Copy is 100% free and open source. No hidden costs, no premium versions.
Does it collect my data?
No. All data stays on your device. No tracking, no external requests, no data collection.
Which browsers are supported?
Works on Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, Vivaldi, and all Chromium-based browsers.
Can I customize the button?
Yes! Toggle sound effects, notifications, dark mode, and more from the popup settings.
How do I clear copy history?
Open the extension popup, go to the History tab, and click "Clear History".
Does it work on any website?
Yes! Works on any website where you can select text.
