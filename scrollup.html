Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

Which browsers are supported?
ScrollUp is now available on Microsoft Edge and Firefox! Chrome version is coming soon. You can also manually install on any Chromium browser via developer mode.
How do I get the Edge or Firefox add-on?
Just click the "Install for Edge" or "Install for Firefox" buttons above. You'll be redirected to the official store for a one-click installation.
How does the resize slider work?
Open settings (gear icon) and drag the slider from 32px to 96px. Button size, icon scale & radius adjust in real time.
Does it track my browsing activity?
No. ScrollUp has zero tracking, no analytics, and only requests storage permission to save your settings. Full privacy.
Can I change scroll direction?
Yes! Toggle between scroll to top (▲) or scroll to bottom (▼) — ideal for chat apps or long comment sections.
