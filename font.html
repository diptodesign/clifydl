Frequently asked questions

Can I use fonts for commercial projects?

Yes, all our licenses include commercial use rights. The scope depends on the license type.

What's the difference between licenses?

Desktop is for installed use, Web for websites, and Enterprise for apps and large-scale deployments.

Can I upgrade my license later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time — we'll credit the difference.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students get 40% off any license with valid verification.
