Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ScrollUp

Is ScrollUp really free?

Yes! ScrollUp is completely free and open source. No paid tiers, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. If you find it useful, consider keeping the footer credit visible to support development.

Does it work on all websites?

ScrollUp works on every website you visit! It's injected as a content script and appears on all pages. Some sites with strict CSP might block it, but that's rare.

How do I customize the button?

Click the small ⚙️ gear icon that appears near the scroll button. A beautiful settings panel will open where you can adjust position, offsets, toggle reverse scroll, and show/hide the footer credit. All changes save automatically.

What's the "Reverse Scroll" feature?

When enabled, the button changes from ▲ (scroll to top) to ▼ (scroll to bottom). This is perfect for jumping to comments, footers, or the end of long articles. The button icon changes to reflect the current behavior.

Does ScrollUp collect my data?

No! ScrollUp works completely locally. Your settings are saved in your browser's localStorage and never leave your computer. No tracking, no analytics, no data collection.

How do I remove the footer credit?

Open the settings panel (click the ⚙️ gear) and uncheck "Show footer credit". The credit will disappear immediately. However, we'd appreciate if you keep it visible to support development!
