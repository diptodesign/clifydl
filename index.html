Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Clify YouTube Shorts Blocker

Is Clify really 100% open source and safe to use?

Yes! All code is publicly available on GitHub. No obfuscation, no tracking, no data ever leaves your browser. You can audit every line of code.

Will Clify slow down my YouTube browsing?

Zero performance impact — it hides elements after page load with no background scripts. Your YouTube experience remains fast and smooth.

Why isn't Clify available on the Chrome Web Store?

Due to YouTube's restrictive policies on content blockers. Manual install ensures we maintain full transparency and never collect your data.

How do I update Clify to the latest version?

Download the latest ZIP, extract it, go to chrome://extensions and click "Reload" on the Clify card. Your settings will be preserved.

Can I block specific YouTube channels or keywords?

Absolutely! The keyword filter works with channel names, video titles, and custom patterns. Regex support is available for power users.

Does Clify work on mobile browsers?

It works on Kiwi Browser for Android (which supports Chrome extensions). iOS support is limited due to Apple's extension policies.
