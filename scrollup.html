FAQ

answers from the extension itself

Is it really free?
Yes, forever. Open source, no ads, no tracking.
How to change position?
Click the small ⚙️ gear next to the scroll button → choose one of four corners.
What is reverse scroll?
Button scrolls to bottom (▼) instead of top (▲). Useful for comments.
Can I hide the footer credit?
Yes, uncheck “show branding” in settings. But we’d appreciate your support!
