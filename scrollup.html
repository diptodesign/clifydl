Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about ScrollUp

Is ScrollUp available on Edge and Opera stores?
Coming soon! ScrollUp 3.6 has been prepared for both Microsoft Edge Add-ons and Opera Addons. While waiting for approval, you can install manually via developer mode.
How do I resize the button?
Click the settings gear (⚙️) next to the scroll button, then drag the size slider from 32px to 96px. The preview updates in real-time!
Does it track my browsing activity?
No. ScrollUp has zero tracking, no analytics, and only requests storage permission to save your settings. Your privacy is fully respected.
What browsers are supported?
Chrome, Edge, Opera, Brave, Vivaldi, and any Chromium-based browser. Firefox support is planned for future releases.
Is it really free?
Yes! ScrollUp is 100% free and open source under MIT license. No premium features, no paywalls.
