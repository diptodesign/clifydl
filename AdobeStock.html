Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Adobe Stock KiMaster

Is Adobe Stock KiMaster really free?

Yes! It's completely free and open source. No paid tiers, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. Created by Trovant Studio (a Dipto Design brand) for the creative community.

Does it work on all Adobe Stock pages?

Yes! It works on any Adobe Stock asset page (stock.adobe.com/images/..., stock.adobe.com/video/..., etc.). The extension activates automatically when you visit Adobe Stock.

What keywords does it copy?

It primarily copies keywords from the "Similar Keywords" section. It also looks for related searches, suggestions, and other keyword lists on the page. It intelligently filters out UI elements and duplicates.

How are keywords formatted?

Keywords are copied as a comma-separated list (e.g., "sunset, mountains, landscape"). This format works perfectly for pasting into search fields, spreadsheets, or keyword management tools.

Does it collect my data?

No! The extension works completely locally. No data is sent to any server. Your keywords and browsing stay private. The only permission needed is to access Adobe Stock pages.

What if the "Similar Keywords" section isn't visible?

The extension uses multiple fallback methods to find keywords. It looks for related searches, suggested keywords, and any keyword-like elements. If it still can't find any, it will show a helpful error message.
