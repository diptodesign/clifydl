Frequently Asked Questions

About our stock tools

Are these extensions free?
Yes! All extensions are 100% free and open source. No hidden costs, no premium versions.
Do they collect my data?
Zero data collection. All tools run locally in your browser with no external requests.
Will these work on other stock sites?
Currently optimized for Shutterstock, iStock, and Adobe Stock. More coming soon!
How do I request a feature?
Open an issue on GitHub or join our Telegram community. We love feedback!
Why manual install?
Manual install ensures transparency and keeps extensions free without store restrictions.
Firefox support?
Coming in 2026! Currently all extensions work on Chromium browsers.
Can I use multiple extensions together?
Absolutely! All extensions work independently and can be installed simultaneously.
How do I report a bug?
Open an issue on GitHub or join our Telegram community. We fix bugs within 48 hours.
