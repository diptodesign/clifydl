Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

Is ScrollUp on Edge Add-ons store?
Yes! We're officially live: Edge Add-ons Link. Install in one click.
How does the resize slider work?
Open settings (gear icon) and drag the slider from 32px to 96px. Button size, icon scale & radius adjust in real time.
Does it track my browsing activity?
No. ScrollUp has zero tracking, no analytics, and only requests storage permission to save your settings.
Can I change scroll direction?
Yes! Toggle between scroll to top (▲) or scroll to bottom (▼) — ideal for chat apps or long comment sections.
