Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Clify

Is Clify really 100% open source?

Yes! All code is publicly available on GitHub. No obfuscation, no tracking, no data ever leaves your browser. You can audit every line of code.

Will Clify slow down my YouTube browsing?

Zero performance impact — it hides elements after page load with no background scripts. Your YouTube experience remains fast and smooth.

Why isn't Clify on the Chrome Web Store?

Due to YouTube's policies on content blockers. Manual install ensures we maintain full transparency and never collect your data.

How do I update Clify?

Download the latest ZIP, extract it, go to chrome://extensions and click "Reload" on the Clify card. Your settings will be preserved.

Can I block specific channels?

Yes! Use the keyword filter with channel names. Regex support is available for power users.

Does Clify work on mobile?

It works on Kiwi Browser for Android. iOS support is limited due to Apple's extension policies.
