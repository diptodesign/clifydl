🔒 Privacy Policy

Smart Copy Pro Extension
Version 4.0 · March 2026
By Dipto Design

🛡️ Our Commitment to Privacy

Smart Copy Pro is designed with a minimal-data-collection philosophy. All your data stays on your device.

📊 What We Collect

Smart Copy Pro only stores:

  • Copy History (stored locally)
  • User Settings (dark mode, sounds, etc.)
  • Saved Snippets (your custom text)
  • Copy Counter (anonymous total)
  • Sponsor Interactions (local only)

✓ All data stays on YOUR device. Nothing is sent to our servers.

🔧 Required Permissions

PermissionWhy It's Needed
storageSaves your copy history, snippets, and settings locally on your device.
activeTabTo access the current tab when you copy text.
scriptingTo inject the floating copy button on web pages.
notificationsTo show copy confirmation notifications (optional).
<all_urls>To work on any website you choose to copy text from.

💾 Where Your Data Lives

  • All data is stored using Chrome's storage.sync and storage.local APIs
  • Data stays locally on your device - never on our servers
  • You can clear your history, delete snippets, or export your data anytime
  • ✓ No cloud storage, no tracking, no external servers

🌐 Third-Party Services

Smart Copy Pro does NOT use:

  • Google Analytics or any tracking
  • Advertising networks
  • External APIs for data collection
  • Any cloud storage services

Sponsor messages contain links to external sites (Creative Market). Clicking these links opens their websites, which have their own privacy policies.

📅 Last Updated: March 31, 2026. If this policy changes, we'll update the date and notify users via the extension update notes.
