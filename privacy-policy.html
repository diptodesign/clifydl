🛡️ Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is not just a policy—it's built into Clify's core

Clify is designed with a zero-data-collection philosophy. We believe that you should have complete control over your browsing experience without sacrificing your privacy. This extension works entirely locally on your device, and no data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

📊 What Data We Collect

Absolutely nothing. Zero. Zilch. Nada.

Clify collects NO data whatsoever. This includes:

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • YouTube watch history
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • IP addresses
  • Device information
  • Cookies or trackers
  • Any form of telemetry

Your blocked videos and keywords are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage.sync API. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

🔧 Required Permissions Explained

We only ask for what we need to function

Permission Why It's Needed
storage Saves your settings (keywords, blocked videos, preferences) locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser and is only accessible by you.
scripting Allows the extension to interact with YouTube pages to block videos, remove Shorts, and apply your settings in real-time.
tabs Detects when you navigate to YouTube pages to activate the blocking features automatically.
notifications Shows optional notifications when videos are blocked (you can disable this in settings).
*://www.youtube.com/* Required to run Clify on YouTube. The extension only activates on YouTube pages and does not monitor or interact with any other websites.

💾 How Your Data is Stored

100% local, 100% private

🔒
Local Storage Only
All settings and blocked videos are stored on your device using Chrome's storage.sync API.
☁️
Optional Sync
If you're signed into Chrome, settings can sync across your browsers—encrypted by Chrome, never by us.
🚫
No External Servers
Clify has no backend servers. We don't store, process, or have access to any of your data.
🗑️
Complete Control
You can clear all data anytime from the settings panel or by uninstalling the extension.

No data is ever sent to our servers (because we don't have any!). Your privacy is protected by design.

🌐 Third-Party Services

We don't use them. Period.

Clify does NOT use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Any tracking pixels
  • Advertising networks
  • External APIs
  • Any third-party services at all

The extension functions entirely offline and independently. No external requests are made from Clify to any servers.

👶 Children's Privacy

We don't collect any data from anyone

Since Clify collects no personal information from any user, we have no specific provisions for children's data. The extension is safe for users of all ages as it doesn't track, store, or transmit any personal information.
